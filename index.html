About Me

Hi, I'm Vitoria, a Front-End Engineer and UX/UI Designer from Brazil, now based in Seattle. I get excited about both the pixel-perfect details and the bigger picture: how a product is scoped, how a team collaborates, and how the final experience lands with real users.

Through academic and personal projects I have deepened my skills in design systems, front-end development, UX research, and cross-functional collaboration. I love combining creativity and technology to build meaningful, user-centered digital experiences.

Currently studying at Bellevue College and actively looking for opportunities in Front-End Development, UX/UI Design, or Program Management where I can keep growing and contribute to projects that make technology more intuitive and accessible.
